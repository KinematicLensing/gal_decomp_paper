gal_id: the galaxy (subhalo) id in the IllustrisTNG-100-1 catalog
f_disk_2d: fraction of stars in the disk obtained by the 2D galaxy decomposition method described in the paper
f_disk_1d_2comp_1: fraction of stars in the disk obtained by integrating the first disk component of the 1D galaxy decomposition method
f_disk_1d_2comp_2: fraction of stars in the disk obtained by integrating the second disk component of the 1D galaxy decomposition method
f_disk_1d_2comp_counter: fraction of stars in the disk obtained by integrating the third (counter-rotating) disk component of the 1D galaxy decomposition method
f_disk_1d_1comp: fraction of stars in the disk obtained by integrating the only disk component of the 1D method with 1 disk component
f_disk_1d_1comp_counter: fraction of stars in the disk obtained by integrating the second (counter-rot.) disk component of the 1D method with 1 disk component
chisq_1comp: chi-squared value of the 2 disk component model
chisq_2comp:  chi-squared value of the 1 disk component model
bulge_r_1/2	: R_1/2 of the bulge [kpc]
bulge_r_1/2_err: one standard deviation errors on the corresponding parameters
bulge_index: Sersic index of the bulge
bulge_index_err: one standard deviation errors on the corresponding parameters
disk_r_1/2: R_1/2 of the disk [kpc]
disk_r_1/2_err: one standard deviation errors on the corresponding parameters
