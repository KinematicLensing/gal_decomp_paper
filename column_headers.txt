gal_id: the galaxy(subhalo) id in the IllustrisTNG-100-1 catalog
f_disk: fraction of stars in the disc obtained by the 2D method
cos_disk_1: fraction of stars in the disc obtained by integrating the first(thin) disc component of the 1D method
cos_disk_2: fraction of stars in the disc obtained by integrating the second(thick) disc component of the 1D method
cos_disk_counter: fraction of stars in the disc obtained by integrating the third(counter-rot.) disc component of the 1D method
cos1_disk_1: fraction of stars in the disc obtained by integrating the only disc component of the 1D method with 1 disc component
cos1_disk_counter: fraction of stars in the disc obtained by integrating the second(counter-rot.) disc component of the 1D method with 1 disc component
chisq1s: chi-squared value of the 2 disc component model
chisqs:  chi-squared value of the 1 disc component model
bulge_scale_len: Scale length (R_1/2) of the bulge
bulge_scale_len_err: corresponding error
bulge_index: Sersic index of the bulge
bulge_index_err: corresponding error 
disk_scale_len: Scale length (R_1/2) of the disc 
disk_scale_len_err: corresponding error
disk_scale_height: Scale lenght (R_1/2) of the bulge 
disk_scale_height_err: corresponding error
